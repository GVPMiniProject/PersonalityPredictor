Before beginning the exam:

  1. Make sure you have a good internet connection.
  2. Shutdown all other qapplications as they can conflict with the web interface.
  3. Use the latest versions of Google Chrome, Mozilla FireFox or Microsoft Edge.
  4. Maximize your browser window before starting the test.
  5. Minimizing the browser window during the exam can prevent the submission of your exam.

During the exam:

  1. Don't minimise the browser during the test.
  2. Don't click the back button or reload button on your browser. This will take you out of the test.

Instructions for the Exam:

  1. Answer all the questions.
  2. Follow the above rules carefully.
  3. The exam can be only attempted once.
  4. Click Submit button to submit and to evaluate the results.
